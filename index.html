How to Use the Tool

Step 1: Go to Myntra Portal

Log in to the Myntra Partner Portal and navigate to the "Scan & Pack" or "Carton Creation" page where you normally scan items.

Step 2: Check & Open

Manually create your first carton scan (just one!) to let the system "wake up". Then, click the Blue Box Icon in your Chrome toolbar.

Step 3: Auto-Fill & Run

The tool will automatically capture your Carton ID and Vendor ID. Just enter:

  • SKU Code (the item you are packing)
  • Number of SKUs (how many in this box?)

Click Start Requests and watch it finish in seconds!
